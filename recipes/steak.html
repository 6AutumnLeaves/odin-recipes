Steak

Description

Rather simply, you will be hungry no more, and all that you desire shall be on an appropriately sized plate. This is for a medium rare steak

Ingredients

  • A cut of ribeye steak
  • Olive oil
  • Two tablespoons of butter
  • Four cloves of garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Let the steak rest at room temperature for 30 minutes
  2. While this is going on, you can prepare the garlic and rosemary
    • Remove the skin from three cloves of garlic and then crush them. (just enough to be opened)
    • Take three stalks of rosemary
    • That's all! Not a ton you need to do there
  3. After 20 minutes, preheat a pan at medium high heat. Coat with olive oil
  4. Season the steak, generously coating each side
  5. When pan is preheated, start cooking steak. Cook one side for around 2 minutes then flip.
  6. Cook other side for 1 minute, then add butter. When butter melts, add garlic and rosemary
  7. Cook for another minute or so
  8. Let rest after done cooking. This will let it soak in more juices
